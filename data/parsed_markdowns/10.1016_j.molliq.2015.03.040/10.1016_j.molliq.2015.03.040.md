As mentioned before, similar trends were observed for the 350 thermophysical properties of ILs, such as viscosity  [23,24] , surface ten- 351 sion  [25] , vapour pressures  [26,27]  and heat capacities  [28]  among 352 others  [29,30] . Although those studies focused on the physical proper- 353 ties of pure ILs with symmetric and asymmetric alkyl side chains, the 354 two basic conclusions might also be extended to the water solubility 355 on these series of ILs that are: ( i ) ILs are highly structured  ﬂ uids with 356 high-charge density areas permeated by low-charge density regions 357 (the alkyl side chain); and ( ii ) the topology of the nonpolar domains is 358 strongly dependent on the relative volumes occupied by the polar and nonpolar moieties. Which means that, the interaction of water and ILs may take place on the high-charge density part of IL, that is the imidazolium ring. The steric hindrance stemming from the two alkyl side chains connected to the symmetric cations tends to separate the stacked imidazolium rings more than in the case of asymmetric cations. This may explain the slightly lower water solubility observed in the symmetric cations. Thus, aiming at a deeper understanding of the trend shifts observed in the IL – water phase behaviour, we performed additional quantum chemical calculations  —  discussed below.
