# S 4 PGSE NMR  

As described in the Experimental sections, diﬀusion coeﬃcient,  D , was determined from the slope of the logarithmic relative spin-echo intensity ln( M/M 0 ) by varying pulse width of the ﬁeld gradient,  δ . Probed nuclides are   7 Li for Li + ,   19 F for PF 6 –  and FSA – , and  1 H for EC and DMC. An example is shown in Fig. S 7. The slopes of the lines give D .
