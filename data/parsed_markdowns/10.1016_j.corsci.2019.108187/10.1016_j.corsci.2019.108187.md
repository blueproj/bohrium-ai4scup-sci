# 5.   Conclusion  

Based on the above study, the following conclusions can be summarized:  

(1) Corrosion behavior of the N5 superalloy and the two nanocrystalline coatings  with solid NaCl deposit in O 2  + 38 vol.% H 2 O environment at 700 °C is investigated.  The N5 alloy shows low corrosion resistance, and the two nanocrystalline coatings  perform better than the bare alloy, in which the yttrium modified one exhibits the  highest corrosion resistance.
