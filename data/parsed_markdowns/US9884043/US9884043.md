Upon activation by antigen-presenting cells naive T helper cells undergo clonal expansion and will ultimately differentiate in cytokine secreting effector T cells, such as Th1andTh2 subtypes.A third and distinct effect or subset has been identified,which plays a key role in providing immunity to bacteria and fungi at mucosal surfaces (Kaste- lein et al.,Annu.Rev.Immunol.25:221-242,2007).This effect orT helper cell subset can be distinguished based on its ability to produce large quantities of IL-17/F,IL-21 and IL-22, and is named Th17 (Miossec et al., New Eng. Different Thelpersubsets are characterized by the expres- sion of lineage specific master transcription factors.
