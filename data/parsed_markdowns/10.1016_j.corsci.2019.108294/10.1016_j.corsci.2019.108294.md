●The oxidation resistance of Nb-Si-Ti based alloy is improved by the presence of Zr.  

The influence of Zr on high temperature oxidation resistance for Nb-Si-Ti based  alloys has been investigated. Results show that the Zr-containing alloys exhibit an  improved oxidation resistance, with reduced weight gain and decreased parabolic  oxidation rate constant.
